MEDICAL DQ & WAIVER REFERENCE
RSID 4D5E · DoWI 6130.03-V1 CH6 FEB 2026 · AR 40-501 · SWA 5/2/24 · AR 601-210 MAR 2026 · MARP v3
⚠ NOT MEDICAL/LEGAL ADVICE
Categories
UNIVERSAL REQUIRED DOCS DD 2807-2 (CMO) · DD 2808 · 680-ADP · USMEPCOM 680-3A · All medical records stamped/initialed by CMO per USMEPCOM Reg 601-23
1
DQ at MEPS — GC notified. Initiate medical waiver workflow in ERM immediately.
2
Upload docs to ERM — all records in chronological order, CMO stamp/initials on each page required.
3
Route to CMO — ALL records must go through CMO BEFORE submitting waiver workflow. Includes prescreens and resubmissions.
4
Submit to Command Surgeon — USAREC Command Surgeon is disposition authority for all medical waivers (acts for CG USARD).
5
Dual waivers — Conduct waiver MUST be approved FIRST before submitting medical waiver.
Validity: Medical waivers valid until physical expires (not 6 months).
Resubmit: NOT authorized unless original condition changed.
BH waivers: DCS G-1 DMPM is waiver authority — not Command Surgeon.
ETPs: Process without required medical docs NOT authorized.
